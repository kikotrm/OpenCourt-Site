Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

To change your password, follow these steps:
  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. Click the "Recover Password" button.
  3. An email will be sent to your registered address with instructions to reset your password.
  4. Follow the instructions in the email to complete the password change.

To register, follow these steps:
  1. Click the "Register account" button on the homepage.
  2. Fill out the form with your username, email, and password.
  3. After registering, you will receive a confirmation email.
  4. And you will be registered.

To log in, follow these steps:
  1. Click the "Login account" button on the homepage.
  2. Fill out the form with your email and password.
  3. If you forgot your password, click "Recover Password" to receive a reset email.

To edit your username or email:
  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. Click the "Edit" button.
  3. Enter the new username and click "Save".

To permanently delete your account:
  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. Click the "Delete Account" button.
  3. Confirm the deletion. All your data will be permanently removed.

To suggest a new location:
  1. Go to the Contact Us page.
  2. Fill out the form with the location details, such as address, available sports, and schedules.
  3. After approval, the location will be displayed on the map.

To rate a location:
  1. Log in to our app with your account.
  2. Select the field you want to rate.
  3. Choose a score from 1 to 5 and add a comment if desired.

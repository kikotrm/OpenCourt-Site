About Us

We always find the perfect court

At OpenCourt, our mission is to simplify the way you find spaces to play sports. We know that it is often difficult to find an available court for your football, basketball, or other sports matches. That’s why we created an application that allows you to easily and quickly search for nearby courts. With our platform, we want to ensure that you never miss the opportunity to play your favorite sport. We are a young and passionate team dedicated to improving the user experience and making physical activities more accessible.
